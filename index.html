What We Build

Seven Integrated Production Capabilities

A single facility, modern equipment, and skilled operators delivering from raw components to finished, tested assemblies.

Wire Processing

Automated cut, strip, and crimp operations on BOZWANG and equivalent high-speed machines. Precision wire lengths for complex multi-conductor assemblies.

Injection Molding

Twelve injection molding cells producing overmolded connectors, terminations, and environmental seals for utility and industrial applications.

Crimping

Reel-fed automated crimpers, PENNTEC pneumatic presses, and enclosed automated cells for consistent, validated crimp integrity at scale.

Assembly

Schleuniger ultrasonic welding, JCWelec connector assembly, and precision gland and torque stations — assembled and verified to engineering specification.

Automated Lines

Coiling, jumper production, and rotary indexing lines for high-volume, repetitive assemblies requiring consistent takt time.

Quality Control

Dedicated QC inspectors with stop-line authority. Electrical continuity, hi-pot, and pull-test validation on every assembly. Full traceability to engineering drawings.

Disconnect Boxes & Final Assembly

Scalable assembly bench lines where pre-crimped and pre-molded components are assembled into finished disconnect boxes, labeled, tested, and packaged for shipment — starting at four benches and scaling to eight.

Quality First

Quality Is Not a Department. It Is a Discipline.

Every shift has a dedicated QC inspector with explicit authority to stop the production line for defects or specification deviations. We build to IPC/WHMA-A-620 standards — the global benchmark for wire harness workmanship — and validate every assembly with electrical continuity, hi-pot, and mechanical pull-test equipment.

Our Schleuniger ultrasonic welding operators are dedicated, non-rotating specialists. Our Manufacturing Engineers are experts in mold changes, PLC troubleshooting, and preventive maintenance. This combination of standards, equipment, and people is how we deliver harnesses that perform in the field.

Strategic Advantage

El Salvador: Your Gateway to Duty-Free US Access

Under the Dominican Republic–Central America–United States Free Trade Agreement (CAFTA-DR), wire harnesses and cable assemblies manufactured in El Salvador enter the United States duty-free under HTS classification 8544 — provided the tariff shift rule is met.

Altus Energy's sourcing and manufacturing strategy is designed specifically to qualify: we source raw materials from CAFTA-DR and NAFTA-origin suppliers and perform substantial transformation in El Salvador. The result is a supply chain that is both cost-competitive and tariff-free into the largest consumer market in the Americas.
