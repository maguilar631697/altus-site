Our Mission

The components that turn solar panels into power plants.

Altus Energy manufactures the balance-of-system components that connect, combine, protect, and control solar arrays.

Built in El Salvador and delivered to the United States duty-free, our products are designed to reduce total installed cost while improving reliability and lead times for developers, EPCs, and installers.

Manufacturing

Integrated PV Manufacturing.
All Under One Roof.

01

Wire Processing & Cutting

Precision wire cutting, stripping, and preparation for all cable assemblies. Automated lines ensure consistent lengths and strip dimensions across high-volume production runs.

02

Overmolding & Injection Molding

Vertical injection molding cells for MC4-compatible connectors, branch housings, and inline fuse holders. UV-resistant thermoplastic compounds, overmolded for permanent environmental sealing.

03

Precision Crimping

Factory-controlled crimping with gauged pull-force testing on every first piece and every 100th crimp. Custom jigs ensure consistent clamping force and repeatable electrical performance.

04

Ultrasonic Welding

Dedicated ultrasonic welding operators — not rotating labor — producing solid copper-to-copper joints for branch connectors and T/Y assemblies. Zero solder, zero flux, maximum reliability.

05

Final Assembly

Scalable assembly benches for disconnect boxes, combiner enclosures, and complete BOS kits. DIN rail, breakers, contactors, and color-coded leads — integrated and labeled per project spec.

06

100% Electrical Testing

Continuity, hi-pot, and pull-force testing on every assembly. Dedicated QC with explicit stop-line authority. Full drawing-level traceability from reel to shipping carton.

This keeps quality tight, lead times short, and costs competitive.
